    Commonly Asked Questions: Answers to Your St Albans Driveway Worries

    How long does a typical driveway installation take?
    The duration of a driveway installation depends on factors such as the size of the area, the chosen material, and the complexity of the design. On average, a driveway installation takes between 3-7 days.
    Do I need planning permission for a driveway?
    In most cases, planning permission is not required if your driveway uses a permeable surface or drains water onto a permeable area within your property. However, it's always best to check with your local planning authority for specific requirements.
